Pronouns (Multiple)

Pronoun directive

When a persona or character has two or more pronoun sets, an instruction is injected telling the model to alternate between them. This is what makes the model actually use multiple pronouns in its own writing.
Use %LIST% for the formatted pronoun sets. {{user}} / {{char}} are resolved normally.
